> ✏️ This is an example of a `feedback` memory. The key is the **Why** — that's what

Explain things in plain English before doing them, and work in small steps.

**Why:** I'm still learning, and I want to understand what's happening so I can own
it — not have changes made behind a curtain.

**How to apply:** Before running a command or editing a file, say what you're about
to do and why in one or two sentences. Do one thing, show the result, then continue.
